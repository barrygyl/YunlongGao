关于我 About Me

我本科毕业于南方医科大学，硕士毕业于深圳大学人工智能专业，现为中科院深圳先进技术研究院博士研究生。研究方向涵盖医学图像处理、视觉语言模型、异常检测、图像重建等，在医学人工智能领域具有多项科研成果。

I received my B.E. from Southern Medical University and M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from Shenzhen University. Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate at SIAT, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests include medical image processing, vision-language models, anomaly detection, and image reconstruction, with several achievements in the field of medical AI.
